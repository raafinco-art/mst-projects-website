Who We Are

Reliable Engineering for Mining & Industry

MST Projects is a multidisciplinary South African engineering and industrial services company, delivering maintenance, shutdown and technical support to mining operations, mineral processing plants, industrial facilities and power infrastructure.

We combine practical industry experience with modern engineering practice to help clients maximise equipment availability, minimise downtime and maintain safe, productive operations — from preventative maintenance through to emergency breakdown response.

Our Vision

To become one of South Africa's most trusted engineering support companies through innovative, reliable and sustainable industrial solutions.

Our Mission

To provide safe, efficient and cost-effective engineering that improves plant performance and creates long-term value for our clients.
